LifeLog

Contacts – A personal directory to store details about the people who matter to you — friends, family, colleagues, or anyone important.

Capsule – A space to capture your thoughts, reflections, or memorable moments as they happen.

Knowledge – A knowledge base for notes, learnings, and resources you want to organize and revisit.

Micro Actions – A lightweight planner to set goals, track small tasks, and summarize your progress weekly or monthly.

Storyline – A narrative builder where you can weave your actions, memories, and reflections into meaningful life stories.
